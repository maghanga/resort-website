Accomodation

Three kind of rooms to meet all your needs: Junior room, Major Suite with their huge balcony and Family room formed by two connected rooms with a shared bathroom.
All rooms have a/c, minibar, safe, free Wifi, bathroom with shower and they can accomodate a cot.
