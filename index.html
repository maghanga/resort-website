THE BOHEMIAN RESORT

This is the official site of The Bohemia Resort

The Bohemian Resort is a modern state of the art resort situated in the vibrant and serene private island of Bohemia

The Bohemian Resort is the coolest place where you can spend a weekend to the beach or a getaway to break the working routine. Ikigai Beach Authorities and Silverstone Air are now partners to give you the best and most convenient occasion to visit. You will dive in our huge pools with Jacuzzi, you can enjoy the Enlighted Safari or visit the Mafia Canyon and the ancient Ruins of Peris or join one of our many excursions and safari.
