Accomodation

Three kind of rooms to meet all your needs: Junior room, Major Suite with their huge balcony and Family room formed by two connected rooms with a shared bathroom.
All rooms have a/c, minibar, safe, free Wifi, bathroom with shower and they can accomodate a cot.

Activities

What's cool in or around Bohemia? Ikigai Beach proposes excursions and experiences to let you discover the magical Bohemia Island, its treasures and traditions.Plato creek for beach life and snorkeling, Peris ruins for History lovers, Mafia Canyon known also as the Hell's Kitchen with its mysterious legends, one of the best kite surf spot in the country, camels and quad riding and much more.
